Odoo Purolator

Seamless connection between Odoo & Purolator

Purolator Inc. offers flexible shipping and logistic solutions within Canada or to the United States, or international destinations. If you use Odoo Enterprise as your ERP system to manage Order Fulfillment & Shipping operations and Purolator as your Shipping provider, our Odoo Purolator Connector will help you connect Purolator with Odoo. Set Purolator delivery methods while creating Sales Order or display them on Odoo Website, automatically submit order information from Odoo to Purolator and get the Shipping label, Order Tracking number, and Shipping Cost from Purolator to Odoo. Print Shipping label, Commercial invoice, and send tracking link to the customer via email.
